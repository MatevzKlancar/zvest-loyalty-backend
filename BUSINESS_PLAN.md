# Zvest Business Plan & Go-to-Market Strategy

**Last Updated:** December 2025
**Status:** Pre-Launch Phase

## Executive Summary

Zvest is a multi-tenant loyalty platform connecting local businesses with customers through a unified native mobile app. We enable businesses without existing loyalty programs to offer digital stamp cards and points-based rewards at zero cost, replacing traditional paper punch cards.

**Target Market:** Small to medium restaurants, cafes, and retail shops without existing loyalty apps
**Initial Focus:** City-by-city rollout
**Go-to-Market:** Partnership with POS provider (2,000 business network)

---

## Business Model

### Phase 1: Free Tier (Launch → 100 businesses)

**Core Features - 100% Free:**
- Digital loyalty programs (stamps & points)
- QR code integration on POS receipts
- Basic analytics dashboard
- Customer app presence (discovery & redemption)
- Unlimited transactions/redemptions

**Strategic Rationale:**
- Zero friction for business adoption
- Focus on product-market fit
- Build network effects (more businesses = more consumer value)
- Gather data on usage patterns and ROI

### Phase 2: Freemium Model (100+ businesses)

**Free Tier (Always):**
- Everything from Phase 1
- Up to 100 redemptions/month

**Pro Tier - €19/month:**
- Unlimited redemptions
- Custom coupon creation
- Push notification credits (50/month)
- Advanced analytics (customer segmentation, visit frequency)
- Email support

**Business Tier - €49/month:**
- Everything in Pro
- Unlimited push notifications
- Geo-targeted campaigns
- A/B testing for offers
- Multiple locations support
- Priority support

**Enterprise Tier - €99/month:**
- Everything in Business
- White-label options
- API access
- Custom integrations
- Dedicated account manager

### Phase 3: Additional Revenue Streams (500+ businesses)

**Optional Add-ons:**
- Transaction fees: €0.15-0.25 per redemption (alternative to monthly fees)
- Featured placement: €50-200/month for promoted visibility in app
- Marketing campaigns: One-time fee for targeted customer acquisition
- Data insights: Aggregated industry benchmarks for market research

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Partnership Channel: POS Provider Network

**Opportunity:** 2,000 existing POS customers
**Advantage:** Built-in distribution, trusted referral source, seamless integration

#### Referral/Affiliate Program Structure

**Option A: Per-Signup Bounty**
- €50 per business that completes onboarding
- €25 bonus if business upgrades to paid tier within 90 days
- Paid monthly via invoice

**Option B: Revenue Share**
- 20% of first-year subscription revenue
- 10% of revenue in perpetuity
- Recurring passive income for POS provider

**Option C: Tiered Performance Bonuses (RECOMMENDED)**
- 1-10 signups: €40 per signup
- 11-50 signups: €50 per signup
- 51-100 signups: €60 per signup
- 100+ signups: €75 per signup + 10% revenue share

**Recommended: Option C** - Incentivizes volume while maintaining sustainable economics

#### Enablement Package for POS Provider

**Marketing Materials:**
- Co-branded email templates (3 variations)
- Sales one-pager highlighting integration benefits
- Video walkthrough (2 min) of business dashboard
- Case study template (we'll populate after first successes)

**Sales Training:**
- 30-min webinar for their sales team
- FAQ document addressing common objections
- ROI calculator showing business value

**Technical Integration:**
- QR code automatically added to receipt template
- One-click signup link from POS dashboard
- Automated onboarding flow (5 minutes to complete)

**Incentive Tracking:**
- Custom referral codes per sales rep
- Real-time dashboard showing signups and earnings
- Monthly automated payout reports

### Direct Marketing Strategy

#### Phase 1: Partnered Launch (Month 1-3)
**Goal:** 20-50 businesses

**Tactics:**
1. **POS Provider Outreach:**
   - Announcement email to all 2,000 customers
   - Follow-up email 1 week later with case study/testimonial
   - Sales team mentions on every customer call

2. **LinkedIn Outreach:**
   - Target restaurant owners in local markets
   - Personalized messages highlighting free tier
   - Share success stories and screenshots

3. **Local Business Facebook Groups:**
   - Join 5-10 local business owner groups
   - Provide value first (answer questions, share tips)
   - Soft pitch Zvest as free solution

#### Phase 2: Network Effects (Month 4-12)
**Goal:** 100-500 businesses

**Tactics:**
1. **Customer Referral Program:**
   - Existing businesses earn €30 credit for each referral
   - Credits apply to future paid tier subscriptions
   - Gamification: "Refer 5 businesses, get 6 months Pro free"

2. **Consumer App Launch:**
   - Soft launch to employees, friends, family
   - Incentivize downloads: "Scan 3 business QR codes, get exclusive offer"
   - App Store optimization for local searches

3. **PR & Content Marketing:**
   - Local newspaper feature: "Local startup helps small businesses compete with chains"
   - Blog content: "How to build customer loyalty without breaking the bank"
   - Guest posts on restaurant industry sites

4. **Partnerships:**
   - Chamber of Commerce membership
   - Restaurant association sponsorships
   - Business networking event presence

#### Phase 3: Scaled Acquisition (Month 12+)
**Goal:** 1,000+ businesses

**Tactics:**
1. **Paid Advertising:**
   - Facebook/Instagram ads targeting business owners
   - Google Ads for "loyalty program for restaurants"
   - Retargeting campaigns for website visitors

2. **Channel Partnerships:**
   - Replicate POS provider model with other POS systems
   - Accounting software integrations (QuickBooks, Xero)
   - Business coaching/consulting firms

3. **Geographic Expansion:**
   - Identify next target city with high density potential
   - Hire local sales rep or partner
   - Replicate playbook from initial city

## Key Metrics to Track

### Business Health Metrics
- **Monthly Active Businesses:** Businesses with at least 1 redemption
- **Churn Rate:** % of businesses that become inactive each month
- **Paid Conversion Rate:** % of free tier converting to paid
- **Average Revenue Per Business (ARPB)**
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Total marketing spend / new businesses
- **Lifetime Value (LTV):** Average subscription length × ARPB

### Product Engagement Metrics
- **QR Scan Rate:** % of receipts scanned by customers
- **App Download Conversion:** % of QR scans resulting in app download
- **Redemption Rate:** Average redemptions per business per month
- **Customer Retention:** % of app users active after 30/60/90 days

### Consumer App Metrics
- **Total Downloads**
- **Monthly Active Users (MAU)**
- **Average Businesses Per User:** How many shops each user engages with
- **Session Frequency:** How often users open the app
- **Geographic Coverage:** How many cities/neighborhoods covered

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Zero-cost entry:** Unlike competitors charging €50-200/month
2. **POS integration:** QR code on every receipt = automatic marketing
3. **Multi-business discovery:** Consumers browse all loyalty programs in one app
4. **City-focused density:** Better than scattered national coverage
5. **Simple for non-tech owners:** 5-minute setup vs complex integrations

## Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Low POS provider referrals | High | Offer higher incentives, create success stories early |
| Consumer app adoption slow | High | Incentivize first businesses to promote heavily, paid UA budget |
| Businesses churn (even free) | Medium | Improve onboarding, add features they request, success check-ins |
| Paid conversion too low | Medium | Delay paid tier until product-market fit proven, test pricing |
| Competitor launches similar | Medium | Move fast, build network effects moat, lock in POS partnerships |
